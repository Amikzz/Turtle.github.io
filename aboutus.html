Our Vision


At Sea Turtle Conservancy, our vision is a world where sea turtles thrive in healthy ecosystems, and their ancient, majestic presence inspires awe and conservation action.

Our Mission


The mission of Sea Turtle Conservancy is to ensure the survival and recovery of sea turtles and their habitats through comprehensive conservation efforts, scientific research, education, and collaborative partnerships. We are dedicated to protecting and restoring sea turtle populations worldwide, advocating for sustainable practices, and fostering global stewardship of marine ecosystems.
